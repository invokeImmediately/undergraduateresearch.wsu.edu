Research Skills Short Course (RSSC)

This course is specifically designed for transfer students and high-achieving, rising sophomores already at WSU who are interested in getting started in research. Experienced undergraduate researchers will lead the activities associated with this program, acting as mentors and leaders.

The RSSC program is currently being revised. More information about when the next RSSC will be available will be posted soon.
