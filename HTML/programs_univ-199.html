UNIV 199: Research Skills Course

Have you thought about undergraduate research? This class is designed for students who are interested in—but not yet working on—research.

  • Learn about academic research with a faculty mentor
  • Gain skills needed in any research environment
  • Explore opportunities at WSU in all disciplines
  • Prepare to start a research opportunity
  • Improve your eligibility for an undergraduate research award

Open to all students of all majors, with no prerequisites.

Course Details

This 1 credit course is taught in the fall. See the schedule of classes website for more information.
