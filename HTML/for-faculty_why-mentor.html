Mentoring and working with undergraduates on research, scholarly, and creative activities can be a rewarding experience.

The Office of Undergraduate Research provides resources for students and faculty who want to become involved in undergraduate research.

There are several mechanisms through which you can involve undergraduate students in your own research, scholarship, and creative activities. Students can enroll in an independent study research course; check with your department for specific course numbers. This option allows for faculty to be recognized for their work. Students may also volunteer, be paid via time-slip appointments, or receive scholarships to conduct research.
