Cougar Undergraduate Research Experience (CURE)

Since 2007, WSU's Office of Undergraduate Research has offered the CURE, a one-week research fundamentals program designed for WSU students ready to enter their sophomore year in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) disciplines.

CURE will no longer be offered at WSU. Students interested in getting involved in research should consider taking UNIV 199
