2015 Summer Undergraduate Researcher Poster Symposium

The finale of the summer-long research programs for undergraduates at WSU, the poster symposium is an opportunity to talk to fellow researchers and their guests about the wide variety of projects students completed in ten weeks.

This year, the poster session will be held from 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. on August 1 in the Smith Center for Undergraduate Education 2nd Floor Atrium.

The event is open to the public with light refreshments served. Student researchers will also be provided a lunch.

Steps to Poster Success

  1. Work with a mentor to develop and implement a 10-week summer research project.
  2. Complete as much of the research as possible in a timely fashion.
  3. Write and submit an abstract to UGResearch@wsu.edu for the poster session. Submission deadline: 7/10/15
  4. Attend the poster design workshop at 12:30 p.m., July 16 in CUE 405.
  5. Design a poster to be printed at the Large Format Printing Service Center. DEADLINE: 7/22/15
  6. Pick up poster the day before the event.
  7. Get dressed up and come ready to talk about your research.
