Cougar Undergraduate Research Experience (CURE)

Since 2007, WSU's Office of Undergraduate Research has offered the CURE, a one-week research fundamentals program designed for WSU students ready to enter their sophomore year in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) disciplines.

This research tutorial program gives students an idea of what to expect as undergraduate researchers by introducing the jargon and methods of research as well as the opportunity to meet faculty members and visit their research areas. CURE covers every step of the process, from finding a mentor to disseminating their research findings.

CURE 2015 was offered May 11 - 15. The agenda each year features a variety of activities during the week. View the online application now to learn about what is required to join next summer's CURE session!

First-year students interested in mentored research are especially encouraged to apply.
